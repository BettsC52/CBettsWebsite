Education

MSc Industrial Digitalisation

Sept 2019 - Sept 2020

Investigating how industrial practices are moving forward into Industry 4.0, with practical experiences in FDM and SLA additive manufacturing, and furthering my CAD skills.

Through focusing on business practices and seeing how they have been changing rapidly in recent times with the introduction of new ideas, such as Additive Manufacturing and the IoT, I believe there is a wide opening for businesses to take advantage of these and increase productivity and reduce waste.

Currently, I am completing my project, titled Part Consolidation of Multi-Component Assemblies. Analyzing a modern stroller as a real-world example, I aim to produce a referable paper detailing the issues and practicalities of part consolidation. With a detailed methodology to design components that are structurally sound while being lighter and more sustainable, with the hopes of reducing waste through effective maintenance and recyclability.

With my project based on part consolidation to use these skills to investigate how technological advances can be applied to improve industrial manufacturing, both mechanically and how changes to manufacturing can improve business.
